Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: July 11, 2025

Pioneer Inspection LLC ("Pioneer Inspection," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive online experience in accordance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and applicable accessibility laws, such as the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

We are dedicated to making our website accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or technology used. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly experience for all visitors, including those using assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, or other accessibility tools.

2. Accessibility Features

To enhance accessibility, we have implemented the following measures:

  • Ensuring our website is navigable using a keyboard alone.
  • Providing alternative text for images to support screen readers.
  • Using clear, high-contrast visuals and readable fonts to improve visibility.
  • Structuring content with appropriate headings and semantic HTML for better navigation.
  • Ensuring compatibility with major assistive technologies.

3. Ongoing Efforts

We continuously work to improve the accessibility of our website by:

  • Regularly auditing our website for compliance with WCAG 2.1 guidelines.
  • Training our web development team on accessibility best practices.
  • Incorporating user feedback to identify and address accessibility barriers.

4. Third-Party Content

Our website may include links to third-party services, such as Calendly for scheduling. While we strive to work with accessible third-party providers, we do not control their content or accessibility features. We encourage you to review the accessibility statements of any third-party websites you visit.

5. Feedback and Assistance

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us. We are here to assist you.

For assistance or to report an issue, please reach out to:

Pioneer Inspection LLC
Bangor, Maine
Email: info@pioneerinspection.com

We aim to respond to accessibility-related inquiries within five business days.

6. Compatibility with Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to be compatible with commonly used assistive technologies, including screen readers (e.g., JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver), magnifiers, and voice recognition software. If you experience difficulties using our website with your assistive technology, please let us know so we can address the issue.

7. Updates to This Statement

We may update this Accessibility Statement periodically to reflect changes in our practices or accessibility standards. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
